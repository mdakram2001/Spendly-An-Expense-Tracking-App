Privacy Policy

Data We Collect

We collect the data you voluntarily provide when using Spendly, such as expense entries, categories, dates, and any personal details you enter into the application.

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the expense‑tracking service, generate reports, and help you understand your spending habits. We do not share it with third parties for marketing purposes.

Data Storage

All data is stored locally in the application’s database. We do not transmit your information to external services unless you explicitly enable integrations.

Third‑Party Services

Spendly may use third‑party libraries (e.g., Flask, Werkzeug) that are bundled with the app. These libraries are subject to their own privacy policies, but they do not process your personal expense data.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact the Spendly support team.
